Welcome to Glacier Monitoring Nepal

This project monitors the changes in glacial lakes across Nepal's Himalayan region. By tracking these critical water systems, we provide valuable insights into climate change impacts and environmental trends. Explore our interactive maps and comparative imagery to see how these glacial lakes have evolved over time.

Click on the map markers to dive deeper into specific glacier lake data, or use the "Explore Glacier Lakes" menu to view detailed analysis of each monitored location.

About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring clarity and awareness to the evolving landscape of glacial lakes through an engaging and interactive experience. By showcasing changes from 1992 to 2024, the platform highlights the expansion of these lakes and the early signs of potential outburst floods. We aim to foster understanding of the risks posed by these natural changes and encourage timely attention to areas of concern. Designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, the platform allows users to explore complex environmental shifts with ease. It also helps those attending the cryospheric workshop gain meaningful insight into the lives, vulnerabilities, and resilience of communities living near glacial lakes and fills the gap between scientific observation and human experience in the face of a changing climate.
